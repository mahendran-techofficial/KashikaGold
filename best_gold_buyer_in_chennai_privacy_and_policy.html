Privacy & Policy

We are committed to maintaining the privacy of your information. By using this website you are agreeing to terms of this privacy policy.

What information do we collect?

The information you provide is referred as personal information. This information may include your name, address, contact number, email address, and physical mailing address. All information we collect is stored on our highly secured servers.

For above mention purposes, we may have to disclose your information to our group, partners, or third parties. We do not buy, sell, rent, or transfer any of your information from/to the third party without your prior permission.

This privacy policy is subject to change from time to time. To have the latest updates regarding our policies keep visiting our website on a regular interval.

Gold Selling Policy

Kashika Gold does not accept stolen, broken, or fake gold. Selling stolen or counterfeit gold is illegal, and if detected, both the individual and the items will be reported to the authorities. Individuals under 21 years old must provide a written consent letter from a parent or guardian to sell gold.
